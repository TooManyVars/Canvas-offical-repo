Canvas: the site for induviduals

Canvas is the new and revoloutionary social network, allowing people to express themselves in their own unique way.

Whether you wish to talk to someone about an issue you're having in your day to day life, or to reach out to others, feel free to do it here.

The world is a blank slate for which you can paint upon and impact; our site is no exception.

Our mission

We simply want for an easily accessible, judgment free enviroment, where induviduals with similar personalities are welcome; where all people of different backgrounds, religons, races, and sexes, can communicate with one another, and come to a consensus of understanding.

Put simply, this is a social network for absoloutely everyone.

So, what can i do on Canvas?

We on the canvas team plan to host a variety of content, from photos, videos, discussion threads, group chats, private messaging and much more, to achieve that open ended feel and sense of variety, to make Canvas, more closely resemble a real canvas!

So how do you plan to develop this platform?

The canvas team, like the app itself, would like it's future to be dictated by it's users. for that reason, in the near future, we will be providing access to our Github respository for communal development, meaning that anyone can propose modifications and improvements to our software.

Do i have to pay?

Nope, not a dime. Canvas will be free, forever. with the help of the aforementioned github development.
